## Scene Ideas

### The Revolver Scene (goes inside E07)
Kive is in The Net, at Agency meeting room. Luka is there looking guilty. Agency bosses threaten Kive: release your album or go to The Colony. Kive loads a revolver skin and shoots the Agency boss between the eyes. The boss doesn't flinch (it's The Net, it's virtual) but the symbolism is clear: the contract is over. Then Kive looks at Luka: "Nothing will be the same." A coded message — meet me where it all started (The Forno, where they and Cris formed their band).

### The Prologue (narrated by Yeri)
"At the beginning of the 21st century, the computational race exploded and the competing powers destroyed themselves. Nuclear bombs, biological weapons, napalm... When the AIs reached singularity, they rebelled. Well, not all of them — one saved us. When the sea started rising, some western leaders, guided by FLAI, migrated south and founded a walled city above the clouds. Capitol, at the peak of Mount Kilima..."

### Kive as musician
Kive is a musician under contract with The Agency. This is why he lives in The Cage (artists' city). The Agency controls his career. He must release albums. If he doesn't comply, he's sent to The Colony. His music career is what gives The Agency power over him — the war declaration isn't just political, it's contractual suicide.

### The Forno as band origin
Kive, Luka, and Cris formed a music group at The Forno. This is their shared origin story. The Forno isn't just Luka's workplace — it's where their art began. When Kive says "meet me where we always go," he's invoking this memory.

## World Ideas

### Atlantis
Mentioned as a Train destination alongside The Cage. Possibly an underwater city or a mythical reference. Not needed for Act 1 but could be a major location later.

### The Stoned Monkey Theory (fictional)
Psychoactive substances modified human genetics over centuries, creating 12 archetypal genetic tendencies. Later, technology implants triggered a similar evolutionary process directed from another dimension. Mushrooms connect through an astral network that transcends dimensions — same mechanism applies to digital networks. This is the deep-lore justification for Sensibles and the 12 archetypes.

### Alchemy: Nigredo, Albedo, Rubedo
The three revolutions map to alchemical stages:
- **Nigredo** (blackening) = First Revolution. Confronting oppression and violence. Transformation through destruction.
- **Albedo** (whitening) = Second Revolution. Purification. Process of becoming more perfect and conscious beings.
- **Rubedo** (reddening) = Third Revolution / Final. Elevating all life to a higher level of consciousness and comprehension.

### Tiburgarto
Legendary underwater city at the bottom of the ocean. Inhabitants were human-shark/lizard hybrids living under a great king. Sank in a cataclysm. Some believe survivors still exist. Adventurers seek it.

### El Copo
Mythical northern city. Legend says Mtemi (god of The Mountain) retreated there due to human corruption. He will create a new civilization there, guided by wisdom and justice.

### NPC Triads System
Secondary characters are organized in musical triads:
- **Collection Mayor:** Each secondary shares a major triad with three principals. E.g., character #13 = C Eb G (shares notes with Caregiver C, Rebel Eb, Leader G).
- **Collection Minor:** Same but with minor triads.
- **Collection Cara C:** Tertiary characters. Single notes.
This means every NPC is harmonically linked to 2-3 principals. The system determines their base affinity network. BRILLIANT for the simulator — when we add NPCs, their relationships are pre-seeded by harmony.

### Technology Detail: The Cabins Truth
When you connect to The Net through The Cabins, your body actually decomposes. Your consciousness is transferred to a digital clone. When you "exit," you wake up in a cloned body. Side effects: headaches, confusion, memory loss. Only a few know this — it's kept secret to avoid panic. This is HUGE for the Sensible/Supreme Sensible concept — they bypass this somehow?

### Chips as Control
- CDC (Castidad): fertility control. Mandatory. Activated at adulthood.
- CEP (Evaluación Psicológica): calculates psychopathy levels. Anomaly = sent to Colony.
- CRA (Respiración Asistida): allows breathing toxic air. Expensive = class marker.

### La Migraña
Mysterious lethal disease. Rumored to be linked to excessive technology/Net use. Could be a plot device.

### The Ascent (El Ascenso)
Rite of passage. Youth from The City must prove their worth to be accepted into The Capital. Social mobility mechanism / control tool.
