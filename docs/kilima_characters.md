## 1. KIVE — The Rebel (D#, Tragic Hero)

**Lineage:** Radbot (inspired by House of Stuart, Edinburgh, Scotland)
**Faction:** The Resistance (member)
**Location at H00:** The Cage
**Profession:** Musician. Under contract with The Agency (must release albums or face The Colony).

**Identity:** Young idealist. Center of the relational web. His impulsiveness is both his strength and his fatal flaw. Driven by grief and vengeance after Len's death. A musician — former star of The Mountain, now a faded glory (though still young). Under contract with The Agency (must release albums or face The Colony). Feels guilty about Len: believes he was a bad example and that The Agency got to Len because of him. Len is now The Agency's new face, which twists the knife.

**Key bonds:**
- Len: sibling (sister). Her death detonates everything.
- Luka: childhood best friends. This bond breaks Luka free from The Agency.
- Pau: best friend.
- Ali: ex-girlfriend.
- Nora: most serious ex-partner.
- Brais: close friend (unknowingly a spy).
- Freya: oneiric connection → intimate.
- Saarah: former friend.

**Arc in Act 1:** Opens AND closes. 8 steps of the Tragic Hero arc. From grief to revolutionary leader to tragic fall. Declares war (impulsive), causes kidnapping (consequences), declares revolution (peripeteia), shot by Brais-automaton (catharsis). THE REBEL ALWAYS DIES.

## 2. LEN (Helena) — The Orphan (E, Mythological Hero)

**Lineage:** Amsoni-Meyar (inspired by House of Windsor, English)
**Faction:** The Resistance
**Location at H00:** The Limbo (already dead/transferred)
**Nature:** The only Supreme Sensible of The Mountain. No mother (the Orphan). Future of humanity.

**Identity:** Fulfills The Prophecy. Born under a special astrological alignment. Controls all four Sensible strains. Her death is the catalyst for everything. Her consciousness exists in The Limbo. By H51, she develops telepathic abilities beyond The Net (can reach Dev in physical reality). Her arc stretches to the end of the universe — culminates in the Third Revolution when Dev fuses her consciousness with humanity's collective unconscious.

**Key bonds:**
- Kive: sibling (brother).
- Nora: maternal figure (Nora is the mother she never had).
- Dev: romantic partner.
- Brais: reunited in The Limbo after both die.
- Freya: contacted by Freya in The Limbo. Len reads Freya's mind (sees the prophecy).

**Arc in Act 1:** Call to adventure = dying. Wakes confused in The Limbo. Discovers digital existence. Receives Brais. Projects into The Cave. Contacts Dev telepathically (H51). Seeds only — arc stretches infinitely.

**Butterfly Clip:** Her material bond with Air-type Anima. Missing from the fake body. The Agency captured the Anima. Nora discovers this.

## 3. PAU (Paulo) — The Fool (C#, Fantasy Hero)

**Lineage:** Delisse (inspired by House of Liechtenstein, Vaduz)
**Faction:** The Resistance
**Location at H00:** The Cage
**Nature:** Savage (no cybernetic modifications). Cannot leave The Cage due to genetic limitation.

**Identity:** Kive's best friend. Funny, eccentric, but not just comic relief. A Savage (no cybernetic modifications) — cannot leave The Cage because he has no CRA (breathing chip) and the outside atmosphere is toxic. His limitation is economic/philosophical (refuses modifications), not genetic. In Act 1 he is NOT transformed. When kidnapped, The Agency gives him a temporary breathing device. In NB2: if he dies at H62, the mushroom revives him modified; if he lives, he seeks the mushroom voluntarily because he's tasted freedom outside The Cage and won't go back.

**Key bonds:**
- Kive: best friend.
- Nora: companion (kidnapped together).

**Arc in Act 1:** The trapped fool. Watches others leave. Kidnapped. May sacrifice himself at H62 (steps in front of bullet for Kive). If he dies: revived by mushroom in NB2 (fantasy hero death/resurrection). If he lives: modified by mushroom in NB2 but carries guilt of Kive's death.

## 4. NORA — The Caretaker (C, Protector Hero)

**Lineage:** Wictar (inspired by Guggenheim, Basel, Switzerland)
**Faction:** The Resistance
**Location at H00:** The Cage
**Profession:** Medic.

**Identity:** Kive's most serious ex-partner. Through that relationship, became Len's strongest maternal figure. Motivated PURELY by protection — no vengeance, no ambition, no glory. She protects because that's what she IS. As a medic, she has access to the incineration room where she discovers the missing Butterfly Clip.

**Key bonds:**
- Kive: most serious ex.
- Len: maternal figure (the mother Len never had).
- Brais: current partner.
- Pau: companion.

**Arc in Act 1:** Protector arc opens. Discovers Butterfly Clip (H08). Kidnapped (H20). Rescued by Yeri. Testifies at Declaration. Clip secret saved for NB2.

## 5. FREYA (Freya Megami) — The Goddess (B, Criminal Hero)

**Lineage:** Kremel (inspired by House of Romanov, Moscow, Russia)
**Faction:** The Resistance (LEADER)
**Location at H00:** The Cave (her physical base)

**Identity:** Leader of The Resistance. Succeeded Dev. Disappeared from The Cage years ago, has lived in The Cave since. Knows: the prophecy, the ether, the genetic plan, The Limbo's true nature. Contacts Len in The Limbo (H10). Recognizes Kive's voice from the declaration as the voice from her dreams (H17). Oneiric connection with Kive becomes intimate at The Cave.

**Key bonds:**
- Kive: oneiric → intimate → command partners during siege.
- Dev: leadership succession (he was leader before her).
- Len: contacts her in The Limbo. Len reads her mind.
- Cris: mentors her at The Cave.

**Arc in Act 1:** Goddess in her domain. Acts on dreams. Takes command alongside Kive during the siege. Full arc (Arc of the Goddess) is between Second and Third Revolution.

## 6. BRAIS (Brais de Lügen) — The Innocent (G#, Redemption Hero)

**Lineage:** Lahaya (inspired by House of Bourbon, Madrid, Spain)
**Faction:** The Agency (infiltrated — secret)
**Location at H00:** The Cage

**Identity:** The most tragic character. Close friend of Kive while secretly working for The Agency. Dies at H20 defending Kive's people. Consciousness transferred to The Limbo. Body reanimated as Agency-controlled automaton. TWO Brais exist after death: the real one (helpless in Limbo with Len) and the robot (a weapon). His body shoots Kive at H62 while the real Brais watches from The Limbo.

**Key bonds:**
- Kive: close friend / secret spy.
- Nora: current partner.
- Len: reunited in The Limbo.

**Arc in Act 1:** Descent. Innocence corrupted. Reports on Kive (H06). Receives orders (H07). Hears declaration from Agency side (H16) — "Confirm target position." Dies at H20. Body stolen. Automaton fires at H62. Redemption arc is NB2 (from The Limbo).

**Alive in story:** 20 hours (H00-H20).

## 7. SAARAH (Saarah Strike) — The Ruler (G, Anti-villain)

**Lineage:** Solom (inspired by House of Tudor, Pembroke, Wales)
**Faction:** The Unions → The Resistance
**Location at H00:** The Capital

**Identity:** Political operator. Initially connected to The Unions. Enraged by The Capital's occultist treatment of Len's body at the funeral. Breaks with The Unions at H18. Becomes the revolution's speechwriter — crafts Kive's Declaration. Will lead The Capital after the Second Revolution. Marries Luka.

**Key bonds:**
- Luka: attraction → bond → marriage.
- Cris: ally (travel together, bond through shared anger).
- Kive: former friend. Writes his Declaration.

**Arc in Act 1:** Seeds. Union operative who loses faith. Escapes Union HQ (H18). Writes the Declaration (H40). Anti-villain aspects develop in NB2 (morally gray political decisions).

## 8. YERI (Alexis Hieri) — The Explorer (A, Post-apocalyptic Hero)

**Lineage:** Baduz (inspired by House of Valois, France)
**Faction:** The Resistance
**Location at H00:** The Cage (to pick up Kive)

**Identity:** The only one of the 12 who has traveled The Road on foot. Valuable information about the outside world. Skilled fighter. The CONNECTOR — weaves all dispersed threads together. His arc is ALREADY COMPLETE (Prologue / First Revolution). He narrates the prologue ("I was a child. I remember thousands of people at the wall...").

**Key bonds:**
- Kive: ally.
- Group: connector (saves Cris+Saarah, rescues Nora+Pau).

**Arc in Act 1:** Post-arc. Using explorer skills in service of others. BUT: his greatest strength becomes the fatal flaw. When he rescues Nora and Pau, The Agency follows him back to The Cave. First breach in history. Yeri's connecting ability dooms them all.

## 9. LUKA (Luka Karuso) — The Hero (D, Archetypal Hero)

**Lineage:** Giosme (inspired by House of Rothschild, Frankfurt, Germany)
**Faction:** The Agency → The Resistance
**Location at H00:** The Capital

**Identity:** Childhood best friends with Kive. Works for The Agency initially. His turn is FIXED — triggered by seeing Kive broken at The Forno (H20). Childhood loyalty overrides Agency programming. Works at The Tower before turning.

**Key bonds:**
- Kive: childhood best friends.
- Saarah: attraction → bond → marriage.

**Arc in Act 1:** Opens. Ordinary world (Agent) → first crack (Saarah says something wrong, H08) → crossing the threshold (walks into The Forno, H20) → fighting alongside Resistance. Protects the Declaration signal using Agency knowledge (H48). Full archetypal hero journey continues in NB2.

## 10. DEV (Andrew) — The Hacker (F#, Action Hero)

**Lineage:** Estemi (inspired by House of Medici, Florence, Italy)
**Faction:** Independent (former Resistance leader)
**Location at H00:** The Cave (or nearby)
**Background:** Former professional gamer. Child prodigy. Len's romantic partner.

**Identity:** Was The Resistance leader before Freya. Stepped down. Now independent. Operates on off-hours. The only truly variable character in Act 1 — his decision (E09, H35) is the single RNG event. Always says: "a self-centered girl and her justice-obsessed brother." His full Action Hero arc plays out in the Third Revolution (~50 years later) when he fuses Len's consciousness with humanity's collective unconscious.

**Key bonds:**
- Len: romantic partner.
- Freya: leadership succession (she replaced him).

**Arc in Act 1:** Call to action. Notices Net perturbations (H01). Investigates transfer (H08-H12). Finds Len in The Limbo (H35). Decides. If refuses: leaves at H49, Len contacts him telepathically at H51, he returns with Backdoor crew at H62. Too late for the shot, saves everyone else.

**Signature line:** "a self-centered girl and her justice-obsessed brother" (spoken always, accept or refuse).

## 11. CRIS (Rous) — The Creator (A#, Urban Hero / Antihero)

**Lineage:** Valardis (inspired by House of Habsburg, Vienna, Austria)
**Faction:** Neutral → The Resistance
**Location at H00:** The City

**Identity:** Artist and activist. Leads the movement in The City from The Forno (artistic quarter, Giosme lineage territory). Fights for creative freedom and independence from The Capital. Furious at the funeral (H00). Her arc OPENS at the Cave breach (H56) — the Rebel's catharsis is her first challenge.

**Key bonds:**
- Saarah: ally (travel together, bond through anger).
- Freya: mentor at The Cave (H42).

**Arc in Act 1:** Pre-arc. Seeds only. Angry at funeral → travels with Saarah → escapes Union HQ → arrives at Cave → Freya mentors her → first epic moment during the siege (H56). The Creator's arc (Urban Hero) is the Second Revolution.

## 12. ALI (Alizia Shirokeji) — The Lover (F, Romantic Hero)

**Lineage:** Tudoru (inspired by House of Orange-Nassau, The Hague, Netherlands)
**Faction:** Neutral → The Resistance
**Location at H00:** The City

**Identity:** Kive's ex-girlfriend. Artist and activist in The Cage (originally). Knew Len through Kive. Sad at the funeral — personal grief, not political. Her arc is intentionally left open ("free bird"). The Romantic Hero arc will crystallize when the rest is solid.

**Key bonds:**
- Kive: ex-girlfriend.
- Len: personal bond through Kive.

**Arc in Act 1:** Free bird. Grieves privately. Helps the group hide. Present at Declaration. Specific role TBD.
